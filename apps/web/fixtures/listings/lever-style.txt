companies. Around 40 of us work from Stockholm and remotely across the EU;
engineering is organised in three small product squads that own their
services end to end.

- Take part in a light on-call rotation for the services your squad owns

What we value

- Care for correctness — billing bugs are trust bugs
- Curiosity about the whole stack, not just one layer
