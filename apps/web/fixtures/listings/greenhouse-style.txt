Tessellate · Infrastructure · Remote (US) · Full-time

About Tessellate

Tessellate (https://www.tessellate.dev) builds a collaborative geospatial
analytics platform used by climate-risk and logistics teams at more than
300 companies. Our backend indexes terabytes of satellite and sensor data
and serves it to analysts in seconds.

The role

As a Senior Platform Engineer on the Infrastructure team (8 engineers),
you will:

- Own our Kubernetes-based compute platform on AWS
- Scale our PostgreSQL + PostGIS storage layer
- Build internal tooling in Go and TypeScript
- Extend our Terraform-managed environments and CI pipelines

What we look for

- 6+ years running production infrastructure
- Deep experience with Kubernetes, AWS, and Terraform
- Working knowledge of Go; TypeScript is a plus
- Experience operating Kafka or similar streaming systems

Compensation and benefits

- $185k–$225k depending on experience, plus equity
- Fully remote within US time zones; annual team offsite
